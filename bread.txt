CHAPTER 1. BREAD

Recipe 1.1: Basic bread
Ingredieants:
- flour (300g)
- salt (3g)
- water (100ml)
- yeast
Instructions:
- mix ingredients (heavy)
- knead until the dough becomes stretchy (15 min)  
- let rise for 1 hour (another)
- mix ingredients
- knead until the dough becomes stretchy
- let rise for 1 hour
- bake at 425 F for 25 min
